Instabilities along the Axis of the Heliospheric Jets

Ma X, Opher M, Kornbleuth M, et al.

The Astrophysical Journal, 2024, 978(1): 57 IF: 4.9

PDF DOI

We report the discovery and characterization of plasma instabilities along the axis of heliospheric jets. Using MHD simulations, we identify conditions favorable for instability development and analyze their impact on heliospheric structure.
